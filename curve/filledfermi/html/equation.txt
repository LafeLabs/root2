





<h2>Filled Fermi Function</h2>
<p style="color:blue">

</p><p class="bigeq">
$$
Y = \frac{1}{2}(\tanh{X} + 1)$$
</p>
<p></p>

$$
X = g(x - x_0) - X_0
$$
$$
Y = f(X)
$$
    
$$
y = y_0 - G(Y - Y_0)
$$


<p>
    X and Y, and $X_0$ and $Y_0$ in physical units of the System. x and y and x0 and y0 in pixels.  G is in pixels per Y unit and g is in X units per pixel.
